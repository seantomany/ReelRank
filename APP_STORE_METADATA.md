## Description (4000 chars max)

Can't decide what to watch? ReelRank settles it.

Swipe through movies, build your personal rankings, and use Group Mode to find out what your whole crew actually wants to watch — in minutes.

HOW IT WORKS

Swipe right on movies you love, left on ones you'd skip. ReelRank uses a Beli ranking system to build your personalized movie rankings based on your choices. The more you swipe, the sharper your list gets.

GROUP MODE — THE GAME CHANGER

Movie night shouldn't start with 30 minutes of scrolling. Create a room, invite your friends with a simple code, and everyone submits movies they'd want to watch. Then everyone swipes through the full list. ReelRank tallies the votes and instantly reveals what the group actually agrees on — no more arguments, no more compromises.

As the host, you get a live dashboard showing who's submitted, who's still swiping, and real-time progress for every member. Set limits on how many movies each person can add. When everyone's done, results drop instantly.

FEATURES

- Swipe-based movie discovery powered by TMDB's massive database
- Personal movie rankings with Beli scoring that evolves with every swipe
- Group Mode: create rooms, invite friends, vote together, see results
- Host dashboard with live member progress tracking
- Watchlist to save movies for later
- Watched list to track everything you've seen
- Trending movies updated daily
- Sign in with Google, Apple, or email
- Dark mode throughout — built for movie lovers

Whether you're ranking your all-time favorites solo or settling the "what should we watch?" debate with friends, ReelRank is the fastest way to decide.

Download ReelRank. Stop scrolling. Start watching.
